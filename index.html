About ZUZU zone-fs

Welcome to ZUZU zone-fs, the premier dining destination at VIT AP University's Food Street. We bring authentic South Indian flavors to the campus community, combining traditional recipes with modern culinary techniques.

Our mission is to provide students, faculty, and visitors with a memorable dining experience that celebrates the rich culinary heritage of Andhra Pradesh and South India. Every dish is prepared with fresh ingredients and served with warmth and hospitality.
